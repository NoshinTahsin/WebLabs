About The Club

IITDU Debating Club is the home for promising debaters

This Club was formed in 2017. There are 40 debater at present. We arrange internal debating sessions to enhance communication, leadership, oration, and argumentation of the students. At the club a series of lectures, interactive drill sessions, and an end-of-the-year intra-department debate competition are held among the students.

Our Values

MISSION: Our mission is to promote logical thinking.

VISION: Our aim is to empower Academy students with the skills of expressing their beliefs clearly and concisely, public speaking and effective communication in a formal setting, and staying aware of current events in the world. Students also partake in sessions hosted by the Dhaka University Debating Society (DUDS) and learn to interact with students from all over the country. Despite the seemingly serious nature of debates, we never forget to have fun along the way.
